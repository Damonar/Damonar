Now Officially Part of Surge Factory Content!

This collection is particularly special to me because it was the very first preset pack I ever released for Surge. I am thrilled to say that these patches are now officially included in all new versions of Surge as factory content! If your synthesizer is up to date, you already have these sounds built right in, so there is no need to download them separately.

Important Note on Compatibility (Pre-1.6.4)

Surge is an incredible synth, and its engine has evolved significantly over the years. Because of these underlying changes in the settings across different versions, a few of these original presets might sound slightly altered or "broken" in the newest builds. However, if for any reason you are still running an older version of Surge (prior to version 1.6.4) and want to test these sounds exactly as they were originally designed, you can easily get them via the form below.

Future Updates & Send Me Your Music!

If I get the chance, my goal is to fully update all of these classic presets to match the latest engine and release them alongside a brand-new collection of sounds in the future. Finally, as a sound designer, nothing makes me happier than hearing my patches come to life. If you end up making music with these presets, I would absolutely love to hear it. Please send your tracks my way!
